Wedding Planner vs DIY Shaadi: Paisa Kahan Bachta Hai?

Kya aap confusion mein hain ki apni shaadi khud plan karein ya ek professional Wedding Planner hire karein? Janiye kahan bachta hai asli paisa aur stress.

Wedding Planner Ka Cost Structure

Ek full-service wedding planner India mein aapke total budget ka **10-15%** charge karta hai. Agar aapka budget ‚Çπ20 lakh hai, toh ‚Çπ2-3 lakh sirf planner ko denge. Par kya yeh worth it hai?

Planners ke charges do tarah ke hote hain:

  • Percentage-based: Total wedding cost ka X% (mostly 10-15%).
  • Flat Fee: Fixed amount, services ke hisaab se.

Wo aapko vendors ke saath deals dilate hain aur aapka time bachate hain.

DIY Shaadi Ka Actual Stress

Khud shaadi plan karna sunne mein toh sasta lagta hai, par ismein hidden costs aur stress bahut zyada hota hai. Vendors se baat karna, negotiate karna, time par sab manage karna - yeh sab full-time job ban jaati hai.
