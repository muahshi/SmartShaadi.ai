5. Hidden Costs — Jo Artist/Decorator Pehle Nahi Batata

1. Touch-Up Charges

Artist aksar "bridal mehendi" ka price quote karta hai sirf ek session ke liye. Agar design uneven lage toh ₹2,000–₹5,000 touch-up charge alag. Pehle se contract mein likhwao ki touch-up included hai.

2. Henna Quality Upgrade

Organic ya imported cones: ₹500–₹2,000 extra. Specify karein kaunsi henna use hogi — cone type quote mein mention ho.

3. Artist Travel Charges

20+ km door se artist: ₹500–₹3,000 travel extra. Nearby artist hire karo ya travel charges pehle include karwao.

4. Extra Guests Ka Buffer

India mein mehendi pe 20–30 unexpected guests aana normal hai. Last-minute catering 1.5x rate par milti hai. Hamesha 10–15% guest buffer rakhein.

5. Seasonal Flower Surcharge

February–March aur October mein rose aur lily prices spike karti hain. Decorator last minute surcharge add kar sakta hai. Fixed-price contract sign karwao hamesha.

6. 5 Smart Tips — ₹20,000–₹40,000 Kaise Bachayein

📅
1. Afternoon Function Rakho (3–7 PM)

Evening mehendi mein lighting, generator, overtime sab extra. Natural afternoon light photography ke liye bhi best hoti hai aur ₹15,000–₹25,000 bachti hai.

🌸
2. Seasonal Flowers Use Karo

October–February mein marigold, rose aur jasmine saste hote hain. Off-season mein same flowers 2-3x mehengi. Date accordingly plan karo.

📸
3. DIY Photo Booth

Professional photo booth ₹15,000–₹30,000. DIY: fairy lights + dupatta + flowers = ₹3,000–₹5,000 mein same Instagram-worthy backdrop ban jaata hai.

🎵
4. Dhol Entry + Bluetooth Music

Sirf entry ke liye dholi: ₹2,000–₹4,000. Baaki time Bluetooth speaker + curated playlist = free. Full DJ ₹20,000 mehendi pe zaruri nahi.

🤝
5. Mehendi + Haldi Combo Package

Ek hi decorator se mehendi + haldi decoration combo lo — 20–25% discount milta hai. Alag alag vendors se sab book karna expensive padta hai.

FAQs — Mehendi Ceremony Cost 2026

Bridal mehendi kitne ghante mein hoti hai?

Full bridal (both hands + feet, detailed): 4–8 ghante depending on design complexity. Simple: 2–3 ghante. Overtime ₹2,000–₹5,000 per hour charge hota hai — artist booking mein time clearly specify karein.

Mehendi kitne din pehle karwani chahiye?

Wedding se 2 din pehle ideal hai. Mehendi ka rang 24–48 ghante mein dark hota hai. Zyaada pehle karwane se color fade ho sakta hai.

Kya mehendi aur haldi ek din mein ho sakti hai?

Ho sakti hai lekin recommended nahi — haldi paste mehendi ko affect karta hai. Budget tight ho toh morning haldi + evening mehendi ek hi venue mein karo taaki decoration cost bachti hai.

Mehendi catering mein kya serve karein?

Chai, samosa, light snacks + sweets kaafi hote hain mehendi mein. Per guest ₹200–₹500 reasonable hai. Full dinner zaruri nahi — light snacks atmosphere bhi relaxed rakhte hain.
