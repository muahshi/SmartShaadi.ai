Hidden Decoration Charges Most Families Forget

Decorators aksar base quote sasta dete hain par baad mein "Add-ons" ke naam par bill badha dete hain. In hidden costs se bachein:

  • Generator & Power Backup: Heavy lighting ke liye alag se DG set ka kiraya (₹10k-25k per day).
  • Transportation & Logistics: Agar venue shehar se door hai, toh truck ka bhada alag se mangenge.
  • Overtime Labor: Agar setup raat bhar chalta hai, toh labor extra paise mangti hai.
  • Service Tax (GST): Hamesha confirm karein ki quote GST (18%) ke saath hai ya alag se.

Common Wedding Decoration Budget Mistakes

Zyadatar log ye galtiyan karte hain jisse unka budget 50% tak exceed ho jata hai:

  1. Imported Flowers during Off-Season: Jo phool season mein nahi hain unka rate 4 guna zyada hota hai.
  2. Choosing Too Many Themes: Har function ke liye alag heavy theme matlab alag props aur extra labor.
  3. Ignoring Natural Decor: Agar venue garden hai, toh usey artificial structures se bharne ki zaroorat nahi hai.

Wedding Decoration FAQs

1. 2026 mein shaadi ke liye sasta decor kaise karein?

Local seasonal flowers use karein, digital LED screens ki jagah fabric drapes ka use karein, aur entrance gate par focus karein baaki area minimalist rakhein.

2. Mandap decoration ka average rate kya hai?

Average Indian wedding mein mandap ka cost ₹60,000 se ₹1.5 Lakh ke beech hota hai agar aap local flowers aur fabric use karte hain.

3. Decoration vendor ko book karne ka sahi samay kya hai?

Shaadi se kam se kam 4-6 mahine pehle. Last minute booking mein prices 30% tak badh sakte hain.

4. Kya artificial flowers saste padte hain?

Haan, high-quality silk flowers real flowers se 40% tak saste pad sakte hain aur ye photos mein bilkul asli lagte hain.
