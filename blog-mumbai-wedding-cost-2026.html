7. Hidden Costs Jo Koi Nahi Batata

  • GST (18% on services): Catering, decoration, photography par — ₹2L–₹5L extra add ho jaata hai
  • Valet Parking: Mumbai mein almost mandatory — ₹50–₹100/car, 150 cars = ₹7,500–₹15,000
  • Generator Backup: ₹15,000–₹40,000 — load shedding ka risk
  • Security: ₹8,000–₹20,000 — bade venues mein required
  • Makeup Artist Travel: Mumbai traffic — artists ₹2,000–₹5,000 travel charges add karte hain
  • Local Body Tax (LBT): Kuch venues additional charges lete hain
  • Coat Check / Cloak Room: ₹5,000–₹12,000
⚠️ Mumbai Specific Warning: Mumbai mein noise ordinance strict hai — raat 10 baje ke baad music band ho jaata hai. Shaadi raat ke function plan kar rahe ho toh venue ka special permission confirm karo pehle.

8. Mumbai Mein ₹3L–₹8L Bachane Ke Proven Tips

  • Navi Mumbai ya Thane choose karo: Same quality, 40% sasta. Atal Setu bridge ab connectivity easy hai
  • January–March book karo: Mumbai wedding season October–December — January onward 20–30% discount possible
  • Weekday wedding: Saturday vs Tuesday — same venue 25% sasta ho sakta hai
  • Outside catering: Venues jahan outside caterer allowed ho — 30–40% food cost bachta hai
  • Alibaug / Karjat: Destination feel, Mumbai prices nahi — 35% savings
  • AI Vendor Negotiation Bot: Smart Shaadi ka free tool use karo — scripts se negotiate karo

FAQs — Mumbai Wedding 2026

Mumbai mein sabse sasta wedding kahan hoga?
Thane, Navi Mumbai ya Vasai-Virar — yahan banquet halls ₹80,000–₹1.5L mein milte hain aur catering bhi 30% sasti hai. Connectivity ab bahut better hai.
Mumbai mein wedding book karne ka best time kab hai?
6–8 mahine advance booking karo. Peak season (Oct–Dec) mein best venues October mein hi book ho jaate hain. January–March off-season hai — better deals milte hain.
Kya Alibaug wedding Mumbai se better option hai?
Budget aur vibe ke hisaab se. Alibaug mein destination feel aur privacy milti hai 35–40% kam kharche mein. Ferry experience guests ko yaad rehta hai. 80–150 guests ke liye best option.
Mumbai mein wedding photographer ki average cost kya hai?
2026 mein standard package ₹1L–₹1.8L (photo + video + drone). Premium photographers ₹2.5L–₹4L charge karte hain. Celebrity photographers ₹6L–₹10L+.
