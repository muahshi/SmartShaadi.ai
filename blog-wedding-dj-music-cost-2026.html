6. Hidden Charges — Jo Quote Mein Nahi Hota

1. Sound System Alag Se

Kai DJs sirf "performance" quote karte hain — sound system alag se ₹10,000–₹30,000. Hamesha poochho: "Kya sound system included hai?"

2. Lighting Rig

DJ lighting — moving heads, LED wash, fog machine: ₹15,000–₹50,000 extra depending on setup. Basic package mein usually sirf 2-4 par cans hote hain.

3. Overtime After Midnight

Standard booking 6–8 hours ki hoti hai. Midnight ke baad: ₹5,000–₹15,000 per hour overtime charge. Pehle se extended hours negotiate kar lo.

4. Out-of-City Travel

Doosre city se DJ/band: travel + accommodation + local transport = ₹10,000–₹40,000 extra. Local vendor hire karo — same quality, zyaada savings.

5. Generator Charges

Agar venue mein dedicated power supply nahi hai, generator rent: ₹8,000–₹20,000 extra. Venue se pehle confirm karo power capacity.

7. Smart Tips — ₹30,000+ Kaise Bachayein

📍
1. Local Vendor Prefer Karo

Out-of-city DJ glamorous lagta hai but travel + stay = ₹15,000–₹40,000 extra. Bhopal ka DJ Bhopal mein, Delhi ka DJ Delhi mein — same quality, drastically less cost.

📝
2. All-Inclusive Written Contract

Contract mein mention karo: sound system, lighting, number of hours, overtime rate, cancellation policy, advance amount. Verbal agreement = future surprises.

🔄
3. Combo Package — Band + DJ

Same vendor se baraat band + reception DJ combo lo — 15–20% discount milta hai. Coordination bhi better hoti hai kyunki ek hi team hai.

🕐
4. Weekday ya Off-Season Book Karo

November–February peak season hai. March–August mein same DJ 20–30% discount deta hai. Weekday booking par bhi negotiate karo.

🎵
5. Ceremony Ke Liye Shehnai — Reception Ke Liye DJ

Phere aur rituals ke liye live orchestra waste hai — shehnai ₹8,000–₹12,000 mein perfect. DJ sirf reception/sangeet ke liye — yeh split approach ₹40,000–₹60,000 bachata hai.

FAQs — Wedding DJ & Music 2026

DJ ya Band — kya lena chahiye baraat ke liye?

Baraat ke liye brass band traditional aur energetic hota hai — ghoodi ke saath walking baraat ke liye best. Agar bus/car baraat hai toh dhol + speakers kaafi hain (₹8,000–₹15,000). DJ baraat mein practical nahi — setup time zyada aur walking awkward hoti hai.

DJ kitne ghante ke liye book karein?

Standard reception: 4–6 ghante kaafi hote hain. Evening 7 PM se 1 AM = 6 hours. Overtime avoid karne ke liye function time clearly fix karo. Zyaadatar DJs 6-hour package offer karte hain — usse hi negotiate karo.

DJ ka advance kitna dena chahiye?

20–30% advance booking ke time, remaining event pe ya 1 din pehle. 50% se zyaada advance kabhi mat do bina strong contract ke. Cancellation clause must hona chahiye dono sides ke liye.

Kya DJ apna sound system laata hai?

Depends on DJ aur package. Budget DJs aksar basic setup laate hain. Premium DJs usually full setup laate hain. Hamesha confirm karo ki quote mein sound system include hai ya nahi — aur kaun sa brand/quality equipment use hoga.
