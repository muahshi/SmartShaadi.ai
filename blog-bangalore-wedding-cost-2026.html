6. Hidden Costs — Bangalore Specific

  • Traffic ka factor: Bangalore traffic notorious hai — makeup artists, photographers 2–3 ghante pehle travel start karte hain aur ₹2,000–₹5,000 travel surcharge lete hain
  • AC charges: Bangalore weather unpredictable — indoor AC venues ₹20,000–₹50,000 extra for full-day cooling
  • BBMP permit: Outdoor weddings ke liye BBMP permission — ₹5,000–₹15,000 + paperwork
  • GST 18%: Har service par — ₹1.5L–₹4L extra
  • Generator: ₹12,000–₹30,000
  • Parking: Bangalore mein major issue — paid parking ₹8,000–₹20,000

7. ₹2L–₹6L Bachane Ke Bangalore-Specific Tips

  • Kanakapura Road / Mysore Road resorts: City se 45 min, 40% sasta, nature backdrop
  • February–April book karo: Bangalore peak season November–January hai
  • South Indian menu + hybrid: Sadhya style base + 2–3 North Indian counters = 25% food savings
  • Sunday vs Saturday: Same venue Sunday mein 15–20% discount
  • Coorg micro-destination: 80 guests ke liye best value destination wedding in India
  • AI Negotiation Bot: Free scripts se Bangalore vendors se 15–20% negotiate karo

FAQs — Bangalore Wedding 2026

Bangalore mein best wedding season kaunsa hai?
October se December peak season hai — weather perfect hota hai. Budget ke liye February–April best hai — off-season mein 20–30% discount milta hai.
Whitefield ya Koramangala — kahan sasta wedding hoga?
Whitefield mein venues slightly saste hain (10–15%). Koramangala premium locations par premium price. Kanakapura Road dono se 35–40% sasta hai.
Bangalore mein 100 guests ki shaadi mein kitna kharcha aayega?
2026 mein realistic budget: ₹10L–₹18L (mid-range). Budget wedding ₹6L–₹9L possible hai outskirts venues mein. Premium ₹25L–₹40L.
