2. LED Stage vs. Traditional Floral Stage

2026 mein LED Screens ka craze badh gaya hai. Lekin kya ye paisa bachata hai?

Digital LED Decor

- Background themes ek click par badal sakte hain.
- Varmala ke waqt cinematic visuals chala sakte hain.
- Cost: ‚Çπ80k - ‚Çπ1.5L (Size par depend karta hai).

Floral Traditional

- Real fragrance aur royal look.
- Labour cost zyada hoti hai (Fresh flowers are expensive).
- Cost: ‚Çπ1L - ‚Çπ5L (Exotic flowers like Orchids/Lilies).

3. Flowers Audit: Local vs Exotic

Bhai, yahan dhyan dena, yahan log sabse zyada lootate hain:

  • Genda/Rose (Local): Sasta hai (‚Çπ100-300 per kg). Best for Mandap and Haldi.
  • Artificial (High Quality): 2026 mein "Real Touch" flowers aate hain. Inka rent 40% kam hota hai aur photos mein real dikhte hain.
  • Imported (Orchids/Tulips): Inka price tabhi badhta hai jab season off ho. Hamesha seasonal phool hi choose karein.

4. Day vs Night Decor Strategy

Bhopal weddings mein light ka sahi use hi budget bachata hai:

  • Day Wedding: Pastel colors aur floral hangings use karein. Light ki zarurat kam hoti hai (‚Çπ20k ki bachat).
  • Night Wedding: Focus 'Ambient Lighting' aur 'Cold Pyros' par rakhein. Lighting acchi ho toh sasta cloth bhi mahanga dikhta hai.
