6. Delhi Ke Hidden Costs

  • MCD/NDMC permissions: Outdoor venues ke liye ₹10,000–₹30,000 + time
  • Traffic management: Baraat ke liye police permission + arrangement ₹15,000–₹40,000
  • Winter fog (Dec–Jan): Flight delays — guests ke liye hotel backup ₹20,000–₹50,000
  • Sound ordinance: Delhi mein raat 10 baje ke baad strict — overtime charges
  • Parking: South Delhi mein major problem — valet + parking arrangement ₹15,000–₹35,000
  • GST 18% — ₹2L–₹6L extra on ₹15L+ wedding

7. Delhi Mein ₹4L–₹10L Bachane Ke Tips

  • Noida / Greater Noida: Same quality, 35–45% sasta — metro connectivity ab excellent hai
  • February–March book karo: Delhi peak season Oct–Jan — Feb onward 20–30% off
  • Chattarpur wisely choose karo: 100+ farmhouses hain — comparison karo, best deals milte hain
  • Outside caterer venues: Farmhouses mein outside catering allowed — 25–35% savings
  • Weekday: Monday–Thursday wedding — 20–25% venue discount
  • AI Negotiation: Vendor Negotiation Bot se Delhi vendors se 15–20% negotiate karo

FAQs — Delhi Wedding 2026

Delhi mein farmhouse wedding best option hai kya?
200+ guests ke liye haan — Chattarpur aur Mehrauli belt best value deti hai. 5-star se 60% sasta, outdoor luxury, aur same Instagram-worthy look. Koi noise restriction nahi hoti usually.
Gurgaon ya Noida — kahan sasta wedding hoga?
Noida generally 10–15% sasta hai Gurgaon se. Greater Noida mein resorts best value dete hain. Gurgaon mein 5-star options zyaada hain lekin premium price par.
Delhi mein 300 guests ki shaadi mein minimum kharcha kya hoga?
2026 mein realistic minimum: ₹22L–₹28L (Noida/Gurgaon outskirts). South Delhi mein ₹38L–₹50L. Grand Delhi-style ₹60L+.
