6. Pune Ke Hidden Costs

  • Monsoon planning (Jun–Sep): Pune mein heavy rain — outdoor venues risky, backup mandatory. ₹20,000–₹50,000 extra tent arrangement
  • Lonavala travel logistics: Guest transport ₹30,000–₹80,000 (buses + cabs)
  • PMC noise permit: ₹5,000–₹15,000 for evening functions
  • GST 18% — ₹1L–₹3.5L extra
  • Pune traffic (Expressway): Friday evening traffic — photographers extra travel time charge

7. Pune Mein Money Saving Tips

  • Lonavala off-season (Jun–Aug): Monsoon mein resorts 30–40% discount dete hain — views bhi beautiful hote hain
  • Hinjewadi IT belt venues: New banquet halls competition mein hain — best deals yahan milte hain
  • Maharashtrian menu: Traditional paan-supari thali — authentic + 35% sasta
  • Mulshi Lake resort: Lonavala jaisi beauty, 15 min Pune se — underrated gem
  • November–January avoid karo: Off-season March–May + Aug–Sep mein book karo
