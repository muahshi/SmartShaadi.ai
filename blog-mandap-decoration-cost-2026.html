5. FAQ: Mandap Decor 2026

Q1: Kya mandap ka base rent decoration mein included hota hai?

Nahi, aksar mandap ka structure decorator lata hai par venue ki 'platform' fee alag ho sakti hai. Pehle clear karein.

Q2: Monsoon shaadi ke liye kaunsa mandap best hai?

Waterproof dome mandaps with PVC covers. Inka rent 20% zyada hota hai par ye risk-free hote hain.

Q3: Floral mandap kitni der pehle ready ho jana chahiye?

Fresh flowers ke liye ceremony se 4 ghante pehle. Zyada pehle hone par flowers murjha sakte hain.
