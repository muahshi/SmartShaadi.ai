Best Areas for Efficient Shopping

Har cheez ke liye alag galli hai. Time waste mat karein, niche di gayi list follow karein:

  • Katra Neel: Bridal lehengas ki wholesale pricing ke liye best.
  • Kinari Bazaar: Wedding laces, latkans, aur decor items.
  • Dariba Kalan: Asli sona aur chandi (Jewellery) ka hub.
  • Maliwara: High-end Artificial jewellery (Designer replicas).
  • Nai Sarak: Menswear aur traditional fabrics.

Mubashir's Efficiency Hacks

  1. The Weekday Rule: Saturday/Sunday kabhi mat jayein. Tuesday ya Wednesday ko 11:00 AM pahunchein. Vendors khali hote hain aur zyada discount dete hain.
  2. Day vs Night Shopping: Din ki light mein lehenga ka asli rang aur fabric quality dikhti hai. Shaam ki artificial lights mein dull fabric bhi chamakta hai.
  3. The "Reference" Game: Pinterest ya Instagram se photos save karein, par vendor ko dikhane se pehle unka "Original Collection" dekhein taaki bargaining power bani rahe.
