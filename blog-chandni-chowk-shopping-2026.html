Chandni Chowk Wedding Shopping Guide 2026

Planning wedding shopping in Chandni Chowk in 2026? This historic Delhi market is famous for bridal lehengas, sarees and jewellery at competitive prices. But without smart budgeting, costs can quickly go out of control. Here is a complete cost breakdown and saving strategy.

Best Areas for Wedding Shopping

  • Katra Neel – Bridal lehengas wholesale pricing
  • Kinari Bazaar – Wedding accessories & decor
  • Nai Sarak – Fabric & tailoring hubs
  • Dariba Kalan – Gold & silver jewellery
  • Maliwara – Artificial bridal jewellery

Hidden Expenses Most Families Forget

  • Alteration charges (₹2,000 – ₹10,000)
  • Urgent delivery fees
  • Matching footwear & accessories
  • Transport & parking
  • Extra embroidery customization

Frequently Asked Questions (2026)

How much budget is required for bridal shopping in Chandni Chowk?

For a complete bridal outfit including jewellery, budget ₹60,000 to ₹1.5 lakh depending on customization and gold purchase.

Is bargaining possible?

Yes. On average 20–30% negotiation is common in most shops.

Which day is best for shopping?

Weekdays are less crowded and better for negotiation.

Is Chandni Chowk cheaper than designer boutiques?

Yes, significantly cheaper while offering similar design options.
