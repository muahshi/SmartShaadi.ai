5. Hidden Costs — Jo Travel Agent Nahi Batata

1. Visa + Travel Insurance

International trips mein visa: ₹3,000–₹8,000/person. Travel insurance: ₹1,500–₹4,000/person. Package mein aksar ye include nahi hota.

2. Airport Transfers + Local Transport

Airport pick-up/drop: ₹1,000–₹5,000 each way. Package mein "transfers included" ka matlab sirf hotel-airport hota hai — baaki sightseeing alag. Local taxi/Grab expenses: ₹5,000–₹15,000.

3. Honeymoon Romantic Extras

Candle-light dinner: ₹3,000–₹12,000. Couple spa: ₹4,000–₹15,000. Flower decoration room: ₹1,500–₹5,000. Ye memories banate hain lekin budget mein include karo pehle se.

4. Roaming + Data Charges

International roaming without plan: ₹3,000–₹10,000 shock bill. Pehle se international pack activate karo ya destination pe local SIM lo.

5. Shopping + Souvenirs (Underestimated)

Most couples ₹10,000–₹30,000 shopping mein zyaada kharch karte hain than planned. Budget mein "shopping allowance" explicitly rakhna chahiye — warna credit card pe load hota hai.

6. 5 Smart Tips — ₹20,000–₹50,000 Kaise Bachayein

🗓️
1. Mini-Moon + Big Trip Strategy

Shaadi ke baad 2–3 din domestic mini-moon, phir 2–3 mahine baad proper international trip — off-season mein. Flights + hotels 30–40% saste milte hain aur aap fully settled hote ho.

✈️
2. Tuesday/Wednesday Flights Book Karo

Weekend flights 30–50% mehengi hoti hain. Tuesday ya Wednesday departure se same flight ₹8,000–₹20,000 sasti milti hai. Flexible hona possible ho toh ye sabse easy saving hai.

🏨
3. Direct Hotel Book Karo — Agent Se Nahi

Booking.com, Agoda, hotels.com pe direct booking karo — travel agent se 15–25% sasta padta hai same hotel. 2026 mein self-planning bahut easy hai.

🌟
4. Bali / Thailand > Dubai Budget-Wise

Same ₹1.5 lakh mein: Bali mein 7 din luxury villa milti hai vs Dubai mein 4 din 3-star hotel. International ke naam pe Dubai mat jaao — Bali 2026 mein best value deta hai.

💳
5. Honeymoon Ke Liye Credit Card Rewards Use Karo

Wedding expenses pe credit card use karo — points/cashback collect ho. Travel card pe 3–5% cashback milta hai. ₹10 lakh wedding expenses = ₹30,000–₹50,000 ka travel reward possible.

FAQs — Honeymoon Budget India 2026

Honeymoon kitne din ka hona chahiye?

5–7 din ideal hai. Less than 4 din mein travel ka stress zyaada hota hai. 10+ din ke liye bahut zyaada leave leni padti hai aur expenses double ho jaate hain. Sweet spot: 6 din.

Passport nahi hai toh kya kare honeymoon ke liye?

Passport application karo shaadi se 3+ months pehle. Tatkal mein 1–2 weeks. Domestic honeymoon equally amazing hai — Kashmir, Andaman, Coorg international se kisi bhi taraf kam nahi hain.

Honeymoon planning ka best time kab hai?

Ideally 3–4 months pehle plan karo. Flights 60–90 din pehle cheapest hoti hain. Popular honeymoon destinations (Kashmir, Andaman) peak season mein 2–3 months pehle rooms fill ho jaate hain.

Honeymoon mein kya activities must-do hain?

1 couple spa session, 1 romantic dinner (candle-light ya beachside), 1 local experience (shikara, snorkeling, cooking class), aur unplanned chill time. Over-scheduled itinerary honeymoon ka maza kharab karta hai.
