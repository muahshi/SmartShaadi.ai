1. 2026 Gold Rates: The New Reality

Bhai, 2026 mein gold koi sasta sauda nahi raha. Aaj ke time par **24K Gold ₹95,000+** per 10 grams par trade kar raha hai. Jab aap shaadi ki jewelry (22K) kharidte hain, toh hamesha yaad rakhein ki Making Charges aapka sabse bada dushman hai.

Mubashir's Insight: Log aksar 'Designer' jewelry ke naam par 20% making charges dete hain. AI designs ne is complexity ko khatam kar diya hai. Ab software se optimized designs bante hain jisme waste material zero hota hai, aur aapka paisa bachta hai.

2. AI Jewelry Design: Precision vs Traditional

Pehle sunaar haath se design banata tha, jisme gold wastage zyada hota tha. Ab **Generative AI Jewelry Tools** se aap apne sapno ka necklace design kar sakte hain.

  • Lightweight Tech: AI aise structures suggest karta hai jo dikhne mein "Bhadi" (Heavy) lagte hain lekin weight mein kam hote hain. ₹1L ki bachat yahi ho jati hai.
  • Virtual Try-On: Bhopal ke showroom jane se pehle AI mirror par check karein ki wo haar aapke face cut par kaisa lagega.
