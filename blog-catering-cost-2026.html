Lunch vs Dinner: ₹350 Per Plate Ka Farq

Timing sirf aesthetic choice nahi hai — iska directly catering budget par bada asar padta hai:

  • ✅ No power surcharge

Hidden Charges — Jo Caterer Pehle Nahi Batata

Yeh "surprise bills" sabse zyaada couples ko shock dete hain. Quote sign karne se pehle yeh sab confirm karein:

1. GST (5%)

Catering services par 5% GST lagta hai. ₹1,500 per plate quote par ₹75 extra — 300 guests mein ₹22,500 extra. Hamesha poochho: "Is quote mein GST included hai?"

2. Crockery & Cutlery Upgrade

Basic steel se Bone China ya Gold-finish cutlery upgrade: ₹50–₹150 extra per plate. 300 guests = ₹15,000–₹45,000 extra.

3. Extra Guest Charge

200 guests quote mein 250 aa gaye? Caterers usually 1.5x–2x rate last-minute extra guests par lete hain. Pehle se "buffer guests" ke liye negotiate karein.

4. Staff Service Charge

Kuch caterers 10–15% service charge alag se add karte hain. Isko "all-inclusive" contract mein include karwao.

5. Food Wastage Clause

Kai caterers minimum guarantee maangta hai — agar aap 200 ke liye book karein par 150 aaye, toh bhi 200 ka bill aayega. Minimum guarantee negotiate karein.

5 Smart Tips — Catering Budget Mein ₹1–2 Lakh Kaise Bachayein

🗓️
1. Weekday Booking

Saturday vs Monday — caterers 15–25% discount dete hain weekdays mein. ₹15,000–₹40,000 aasani se bach jaata hai.

🤝
2. 3-Quote Rule

Hamesha kam se kam 3 caterers se quote lo. Competition se automatically 10–20% rate kam hota hai.

🌿
3. Seasonal Menu

Out-of-season vegetables 2-3x mehengi hoti hain. Caterer se seasonal menu suggest karne ko kaho — same taste, 15% kam cost.

📊
4. RSVP-Based Catering

Confirmed guests ke hisaab se order karo, total invite list se nahi. Smart Shaadi ka RSVP tracker se exact count milta hai.

📝
5. Written Contract

Har cheez written mein: per plate rate, GST status, extra guest rate, minimum guarantee, overtime charges. Verbal agreements kaam nahi aate.

FAQs — Aksar Pooche Jaane Wale Sawaal

2026 mein 200 guests ki shaadi ka catering budget kitna hoga?

200 guests ke liye: Budget (₹850/plate) = ₹1,70,000 | Standard (₹1,400/plate) = ₹2,80,000 | Premium (₹2,000/plate) = ₹4,00,000. GST aur service charges alag. SmartShaadi ka Budget Calculator try karein exact estimate ke liye.

Veg aur Non-Veg catering mein kitna farq hota hai?

Non-Veg generally ₹200–₹500 per plate zyaada hota hai depending on items. Chicken dishes ₹150–₹250, Mutton ₹300–₹500 extra per plate add karte hain. Mixed menu (mostly veg + 1 non-veg counter) sabse cost-effective option hai.

Catering advance mein kitna dena hota hai?

Standard practice: 30–50% advance booking ke time, remaining 1 week pehle ya event pe. Kabhi bhi 100% pehle mat do. Refund policy written mein leni chahiye.

Live counters zaruri hain kya?

Agar budget tight hai, live counters skip kar sakte hain — ₹150–₹300/plate bachte hain. Lekin premium shaadi mein guests live counters expect karte hain. 1 live counter (chaat ya pasta) rakhna good compromise hai.
