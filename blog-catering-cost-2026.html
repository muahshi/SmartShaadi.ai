Hidden Catering Charges Most Families Forget

Jab aap caterer se baat karte hain, toh wo aapko base price batate hain. Lekin bill aate waqt ye "Hidden Charges" aapka budget hila dete hain:

  • GST (18%): Catering service par 18% GST lagta hai jo plate rate mein aksar included nahi hota.
  • Waiters & Staff Service: Per waiter cost ₹800-₹1200 (per event) extra ho sakti hai.
  • Transportation: Agar venue city se dur hai, toh transport ka ₹10,000-₹30,000 extra lag sakta hai.
  • Leftover Management: Khana bachne par usey pack karne ya dispose karne ka charge.
  • Live Counter Fuel: LPG aur coal ka extra kharcha.
Pro Tip: Hamesha "All-Inclusive" quote maangein taaki end mein koi surprise na ho.

Common Wedding Catering Budget Mistakes

Duniya bhar mein log catering par paisa waste karte hain kyunki wo ye galti karte hain:

  1. Over-Ordering: Guest list se 15% zyada khana order karna jabki 5-10% kaafi hota hai.
  2. Too Many Starters: Jab guest starters zyada khate hain, toh main course waste jata hai. 4 Starters are enough!
  3. Off-Season Negotiation: Log summer weddings (April-July) mein bhi peak season rates dete hain. Negotiation is key!

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Can I bring my own alcohol for the wedding bar?

Most caterers allow it but charge a 'Corkage Fee' which can range from ₹200 to ₹500 per bottle. Always clarify this in the contract.

2. How much does a dessert-only counter cost?

If you add a specialized dessert counter (like Waffle or Jalebi), expect to add ₹150-₹300 extra to your base plate price.

3. What is the standard advance payment for caterers?

Typically 25-40% at the time of booking, and the remaining 60-75% on the day of the event or a day before.

4. How to reduce catering cost per plate without compromising quality?

Reduce the number of main course items (repeat tastes), use seasonal vegetables, and limit live counters to just two popular items.
