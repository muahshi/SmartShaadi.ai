Day vs Night: The Mathematical Savings

Timing ka impact sirf ambiance par nahi, plate cost par bhi hota hai:

Day Lunch (Saving: ~15%)

Lighter menu, less lighting fuel, fewer dessert varieties. Guests eat 20% less appetizers in heat.

Night Dinner (Premium)

Heavy appetizers, multiple live stations, extensive dessert bar. High fuel and late-night labor costs.
