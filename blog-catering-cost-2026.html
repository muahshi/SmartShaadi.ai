Day vs Night: The Cost of Timing

Lunch (Day)

Focus on light flavors and chilled beverages. Lower fuel costs for heating and fewer dessert counters often result in a 15% saving.

Dinner (Night)

Requires heavy lighting, hot dessert stations, and extensive appetizer rounds. Expect higher fuel and labor overheads.

The Hidden Bill: What They Don't Quote

The base price is just the beginning. Watch out for these 2026 surcharges:

01

GST (18%): Mandatory on all catering services. A ₹2,000 plate is actually ₹2,360.

02

Labor Ratio: Premium service (1:10 staff-to-guest) adds ₹150-₹300 per plate.

03

Generator/Fuel: Live stations using high-pressure burners often bill fuel separately.
