5. Hidden Charges — Jo Artist Pehle Nahi Batata

1. Touch-Up / Retouch Charges

Artist aksar "bridal makeup" quote karta hai sirf main function ke liye. Evening reception ya baraat ke liye retouch ₹3,000–₹8,000 extra. Contract mein clearly likhwao — kitne touch-ups included hain.

2. Premium Product Upgrade

Quote mein "imported products" bolte hain, baad mein bolte hain "MAC/Charlotte Tilbury extra charge hai" — ₹3,000–₹10,000 extra. Pehle hi puchho — kaunse specific brands use honge.

3. Artist Travel + Assistant Charges

Bahar se artist bulaya: ₹1,000–₹5,000 travel. Artist ke saath 2 assistants: ₹2,000–₹6,000 extra. Pehle hi all-inclusive price confirm karo.

4. Early Morning Surcharge

Agar makeup 5–7 AM tak karwana hai, ₹2,000–₹5,000 early morning charge hota hai. Wedding timing ke hisaab se ye plan karo.

5. Saree Draping + Hair Extra

Makeup package mein saree draping aur elaborate hairstyle aksar include nahi hoti. Hair: ₹3,000–₹12,000, Draping: ₹1,500–₹4,000 alag. Ek hi artist se combo lo — 15–20% sasta padta hai.

6. 5 Smart Tips — ₹15,000–₹30,000 Kaise Bachayein

🧪
1. Trial Mandatory Karo — Aur Negotiate Karo

Trial mein ₹2,000–₹5,000 lagti hai jo final package mein adjust hoti hai. Agar artist trial offer nahi karta — immediately alag artist dhundho. Trial save karta hai emergency on wedding day.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦
2. Family Combo Package Lo

5–6 family members ka makeup ek hi artist se — 20–30% discount milta hai. Alag alag artists hire karne se coordination problem + zyaada cost. Ek artist, ek deal.

📍
3. Local Artist Ko Prefer Karo

Doosre city se artist bulana = travel + stay + food — ₹5,000–₹15,000 extra. Local professional artist same quality deta hai bina travel baggage ke. Portfolio carefully verify karo.

📝
4. Written Contract — Har Cheez Listed

Products ka brand naam, touch-up count, functions covered, travel included/excluded — sab contract mein likhwao. Verbal promises wedding industry mein kuch nahi hoti.

🗓️
5. Off-Season Booking — 4+ Months Pehle

November–February mein good artists book ho jaate hain. 4–6 months advance booking pe 10–15% early bird discount milta hai. Last minute booking mein premium 20–30% zyaada lagta hai.

FAQs — Dulhan Makeup Cost 2026

Makeup artist ko advance kitna dena chahiye?

20–30% advance par booking confirm hoti hai. Baaki amount shaadi ke din ya 1 day pehle dein. Poora advance mat dein — cancellation mein problem hogi. Agar artist 50%+ advance demand kare, red flag hai.

Ek din mein kitne functions ka makeup ek artist kar sakta hai?

Ek artist typically: Mehendi (light makeup) + Haldi (minimal) + Shaadi (bridal) — 3 functions manage kar sakta hai agar sab ek hi din hain. Reception ke liye touch-up hoti hai. Separate events mein assistant bhi saath hona chahiye.

Kya self-makeup karni chahiye budget bachane ke liye?

Agar aap professional makeup course kiya hai ya genuinely skilled hain toh self-makeup ho sakti hai. Lekin most brides ke liye — stress + time + results ke hisaab se — professional better value deta hai. Compromise: mehendi/haldi pe self, sirf shaadi ke din professional lo.

Skin type ke hisaab se kaunsa foundation best hai?

Oily skin: Matte finish airbrush best hai — long-lasting. Dry skin: Dewy finish traditional ya HD. Sensitive skin: Artist ko pehle se products ka list share karwao — patch test zaroori hai 1 week pehle.
