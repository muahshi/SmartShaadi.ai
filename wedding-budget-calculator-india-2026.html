Wedding Budget FAQs

How much does an Indian wedding cost in 2026?

An average wedding costs ₹20,00,000 for 200 guests in a Tier-2 city like Bhopal. In Mumbai or Delhi, this can go up to ₹50,00,000.

Can I plan a wedding under ₹5 lakh?

Yes. By limiting the guest list to 50 people, choosing a government guest house or community hall, and opting for local seasonal flowers, a beautiful wedding under ₹5L is achievable.

Is a destination wedding cheaper than a local one?

Usually, no. Destination weddings include travel and 2-day stay for all guests, which increases the budget by at least 40-50% compared to a local wedding.
