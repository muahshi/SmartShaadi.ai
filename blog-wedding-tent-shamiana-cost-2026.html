5. Hidden Charges — Jo Contractor Pehle Nahi Batata

1. Generator (DG Set) Charges

Tent quote mein bijli nahi hoti usually. Generator: ₹8,000–₹25,000/day. Pehle hi puchho — kya mains connection possible hai? DG set cost quote mein include karwao.

2. Flooring / Carpet

Open ground mein flooring mandatory. Carpet: ₹8,000–₹20,000. Wooden flooring (luxury): ₹25,000–₹60,000. "Flooring included" specifically likhwao quote mein.

3. Overtime Charges

Standard booking: 6–8 hours. Indian weddings late chalti hain. Overtime: ₹3,000–₹8,000/hour. Contract mein 11 PM tak include karwao.

4. Dismantling / Removal Charges

Setup charge alag, dismantling alag — ₹5,000–₹15,000 extra. All-inclusive mein setup + stay + dismantling sab maango.

5. Extra Lighting & Decor Uplift

Base quote mein sirf basic bulbs. Fairy lights, chandeliers, LED panels sab extra — contractor baad mein ₹15,000–₹40,000 extra charge karta hai.

6. 5 Smart Tips — ₹20,000–₹60,000 Kaise Bachayein

📝
1. All-Inclusive Written Quote Maango

Generator, flooring, lighting, chairs, tables, setup, dismantling — har cheez explicitly listed honi chahiye. Verbal promise = guaranteed surprise bill.

🗓️
2. Sardi Mein Shaadi — AC Tent Bachao

November–February mein AC tent ki zaroorat nahi — ₹60,000–₹1,50,000 automatically bach jaata hai.

💡
3. Khud Ki Fairy Lights Khareedo

Online se fairy lights: ₹3,000–₹7,000. Contractor se rent: ₹15,000–₹30,000. Ek baar kharid lo — multiple events mein use hogi.

🕐
4. Contract Mein Late End Time Likhwao

12 AM tak ka time include karwao — overtime ₹3,000–₹8,000/hour bachega. India mein functions late chalti hain — ye reality hai.

🤝
5. Tent + Decoration Combo Deal Lo

Ek hi vendor se tent + decoration — alag alag se 25–35% zyaada padta hai. Combo mein coordination bhi better.

FAQs — Wedding Tent & Shamiana Cost 2026

Tent kitne din pehle book karna chahiye?

Peak season (Oct–Feb) mein 2–3 months pehle. Good contractors jaldi full ho jaate hain. Off-season mein 4–6 weeks advance kaafi. Early booking pe 10–15% discount bhi milta hai.

Barsat mein tent safe hai?

Waterproof tent hote hain lekin heavy rain mein guarantee nahi. Monsoon wedding mein: (1) Waterproof certificate maango, (2) Backup indoor venue confirm karo, (3) Contract mein rain damage clause check karo.

Ghar ke aangan mein tent lag sakta hai?

Haan — minimum 800 sq ft open space chahiye basic setup ke liye. Neighbours + local authority se permission leni pad sakti hai. Contractor ko space ka photo/dimensions pehle bhejo.

Tent mein bathroom ki arrangement kaise hoti hai?

Premium contractors portable luxury washroom dete hain — ₹8,000–₹20,000 extra. Grand outdoor events mein portable washroom must hai — guests ka experience important hai.
