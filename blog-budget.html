How to Plan a Smart Wedding Budget (AI Strategy)

Shaadi mein sabse bada stress 'Budget' ka hota hai. SmartShaadi AI ki madad se aap apne kharchon ko 5 categories mein divide kar sakte hain:

1. The 40% Rule: Venue & Catering

Aapka 40% budget sirf venue aur khane par hona chahiye. Agar aap isse zyada kharch karte hain, to baki cheezon mein compromise karna padega.

2. The 20% Rule: Outfits & Jewelry

Jewelry ek investment hai, lekin wedding outfits ko manage karna zaruri hai. AI suggest karta hai ki aap rent aur purchase ka mix rakhein.

3. The 15% Rule: Decoration & Photography

Photos lifetime rehti hain, isliye ek achha photographer zaruri hai. Decoration mein modern minimalist themes use karke aap paise bacha sakte hain.

Conclusion

Smart planning ka matlab kam kharch karna nahi, balki sahi jagah kharch karna hai. SmartShaadi.ai ka form bhariye aur apna personalized budget paiye!
