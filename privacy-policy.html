Smart Shaadi AI ("hum", "hamara", "website") ek free AI wedding planning platform hai jo Indian couples ke liye banaya gaya hai. Yeh Privacy Policy explain karti hai ki hum aapka personal data kaise collect, use, aur protect karte hain jab aap smartshaadi.online visit karte hain.

1 Information We Collect

Hum do tarah ka data collect karte hain:

A) Information You Provide Directly:

  • Contact form mein diya gaya naam aur email address
  • Wedding planning tools mein enter kiya gaya data (budget, guest count, wedding date, city)
  • AI chatbot ke saath kiya gaya conversation

B) Information Collected Automatically:

  • IP address aur approximate location (city/country level)
  • Browser type, device type, aur operating system
  • Pages visited, time spent, aur referring URLs
  • Cookies aur similar tracking technologies

2 How We Use Your Information

Aapka data hum sirf in purposes ke liye use karte hain:

  • AI tools aur wedding planning features provide karne ke liye
  • Contact form submissions ka jawab dene ke liye
  • Website performance improve karne ke liye (analytics)
  • Relevant advertisements display karne ke liye (Google AdSense)
  • Legal obligations comply karne ke liye

⚠️ Hum aapka personal data kabhi bhi sell nahi karte kisi third party ko. Hum aapka data kisi bhi marketing company ko transfer nahi karte bina aapki permission ke.

3 Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Hamari website cookies use karti hai. Cookies chhote text files hain jo aapke browser mein store hote hain.

Hum in cookies ka use karte hain:

  • Essential Cookies: Website properly kaam kare is ke liye zaroori
  • Analytics Cookies: Google Analytics ke through traffic analyze karne ke liye
  • Advertising Cookies: Google AdSense ke through relevant ads dikhane ke liye

Aap apne browser settings se cookies disable kar sakte hain, lekin isse website ka kuch functionality affect ho sakti hai.

4 Google AdSense & Third-Party Ads

Smart Shaadi AI Google AdSense use karta hai website pe advertisements display karne ke liye. Google AdSense hum par ek trusted advertising partner hai.

Important Google AdSense Information:

  • Google aapki website visits ke basis par personalized ads dikhata hai
  • Google DoubleClick cookie use karta hai aapke browser mein relevant ads serve karne ke liye
  • Aap Google's opt-out page visit karke personalized ads se opt-out kar sakte hain: www.google.com/settings/ads
  • Google ki Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

Hum Google AdSense ke saath data share karte hain sirf advertising purposes ke liye, aur ye Google ki own privacy policy ke under governed hai.

Hamari website kuch third-party services use karti hai:

  • Google Analytics: Website traffic aur user behavior analyze karne ke liye
  • Google AdSense: Advertising revenue ke liye

6 Data Retention

Hum aapka data tab tak retain karte hain jab tak:

  • Planning tool data: Ye data sirf aapke browser mein locally store hota hai, hamari servers par nahi

Aap kisi bhi time hamein email karke apna data delete karne ki request kar sakte hain.
