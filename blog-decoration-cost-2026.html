Day Wedding vs Night Wedding Decor

Aapki shaadi ka waqt aapke budget ko 30% tak influence kar sakta hai. 2026 ke trends ke mutabiq:

Day Decor (Morning)

Day weddings mein lighting ka kharcha minimal hota hai, lekin floral density badhani padti hai. Pastel themes aur fabric drapes yahan saste aur sundar lagte hain.

Night Decor (Evening)

Yahan lights hi sab kuch hain. Kam flowers use karke sirf "Warm White" ya "Amber" lights se ek royal mahal wala look create kiya ja sakta hai.

Hidden Costs You Must Track

  • Genset & Power: Luxury LED installs consume massive power. Backup can cost an extra ₹25,000.
  • GST (18%): On a ₹10L quote, the tax alone is ₹1.8 Lakhs. Always ask for 'all-inclusive' quotes.
