🎵 Perfect Wedding Playlist Ke 5 Golden Tips

  • 🎧
    DJ ko pehle se list dena zaroori hai — Wedding se kam se kam 1 week pehle DJ ko complete list de do. Last-minute request pe DJ ka preparation nahi hota aur quality suffer karti hai.
  • 📈
    Energy curve plan karo — Har ceremony mein slow start, phir build-up, phir peak hona chahiye. Sangeet mein slow romantic se shuru karo aur dance anthems pe khatam karo.
  • 👨‍👩‍👧
    All-age mix zaroori hai — 2-3 classic retro songs rakhna na bhuolo — bade log sab se zyada khush hote hain jab unki era ka koi gaana baje!
  • 💌
    Couple ki entry song special rakho — Reception mein couple entry ke liye ek unique, meaningful song choose karo jo sirf unka ho. Yeh moment sabse zyada yaad rehta hai.
  • 🔕
    Avoid list banana equally important hai — Must-play list se equally zaroori hai avoid list. Kuch songs cultural ya personal reasons se inappropriate ho sakte hain — DJ ko clearly batao.

Aksar Pooche Jane Wale Sawaal

3 ghante ki Sangeet Night ke liye 25-35 songs kaafi hote hain. Ek song average 4-5 minute ka hota hai. Kuch extra backup songs hamesha rakho — agar koi song flop ho toh DJ skip kar sake.
Ceremony-wise clearly separated list deni chahiye. Must-play songs highlight karo. Timing bhi bata do (e.g. "9 PM couple entry"). Avoid songs separate list mein dena best practice hai. Yahan DJ Note button se ready-made professional note copy kar sakte hain!
2026 mein top baraat songs: Kala Chashma, Saturday Saturday, Diljale, Ainvayi Ainvayi, Baaraat Nikaalo, Aaj Ki Party, Desi Beat aur latest Punjabi hits. Dhol beats wale songs baraat mein sabse zyada kaam karte hain.
Vidai ke liye top picks: Babul Ki Duaein Leti Ja (evergreen), Mora Piya, Alvida, Tujhe Bhula Diya, Fareeda, Kabhi Khushi Kabhi Gham, Aaj Sajna Mohe Ang Lago, aur Dooriyan. Slow, soft instrumentals bhi beech mein rakhna accha lagta hai.
Haan! Reception mein especially English/Western songs acche lagte hain — Ed Sheeran, Bruno Mars jaise romantic songs couple's first dance ke liye perfect hain. Young crowd ke liye English pop bhi mix kar sakte ho. Balance zaroori hai.
Entry song powerful, memorable aur couple ki personality reflect karna chahiye. Traditional couples ke liye: Tum Hi Ho ya Teri Meri. Modern couples ke liye: Excuses, Kesariya ya Raataan Lambiyan. Fun couples ke liye: Kaho Naa Pyaar Hai ya Zingaat. AI se personalized suggestion lena sabse accha!
