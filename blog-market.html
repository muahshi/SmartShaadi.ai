Delhi's Best Wedding Markets: 2026 Shopping Guide

Shaadi ki shopping aur Delhi ka rishta bahut purana hai. Agar aap 2026 mein apni shaadi plan kar rahe hain, to ye markets aapki list mein honi chahiye:

1. Chandni Chowk (Lehenga Hub)

Sabse iconic jagah. Yahan aapko 15,000 se lekar 5 Lakh tak ke designer-inspired lehengas mil jayenge. Om Prakash Jawahar Lal aur Asiana Couture jaise bade naam yahi hain.

2. Karol Bagh (Jewelry & Suits)

Ajmal Khan Road suits aur men's wear ke liye best hai. Bank Street par aapko gold aur diamond jewelry ke hazaron options milenge.

3. Shahpur Jat (Designer Wear)

Agar aapko unique aur boutique-style dresses chahiye, to Shahpur Jat best hai. Yahan budding designers ke unique pieces milte hain.

Smart Tip: Hamesha weekdays par shopping karein taaki crowd kam mile aur aap achhe se negotiate kar sakein.
