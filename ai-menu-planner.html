🍽️ Caterer Se Deal Karne Ke 6 Golden Rules

⚖️
Exact quantities lekar jaao — "200 log hain" se behtar hai "200 guests, 75kg chawal, 40kg dal, 15kg paneer" — caterer ke paas exact brief hoga toh woh extra charge nahi kar sakta.
📋
3 caterers se quotes lo — hamesha — Ek caterer ka quote final mat karo. 3 quotes lo, compare karo, phir negotiate karo. Average mein 15-20% difference hota hai.
🥘
Kam dishes, premium quality — 14 average dishes se behtar hain 9 excellent dishes. Guests quality yaad rakhte hain, quantity nahi. Live counters se perceived value 2x ho jaati hai.
📅
Off-season mein 20-30% sasta — Same caterer, same food — April–September mein 20-30% less charge karte hain. Smart families off-season mein book karti hain.
📝
Written agreement zaroor lo — Verbal deal pe shaadi nahi hoti! Menu, quantity, timing, setup, cleanup — sab likhit mein. Yahan Caterer Note copy karo, WhatsApp karo.
💰
Advance 20%, rest on delivery — 50%+ advance mat do. 20% advance, 60% delivery pe, 20% sab theek hone ke baad. Leverage aapke paas rehna chahiye.

Aksar Pooche Jane Wale Sawaal

2026 mein city-wise: Delhi/Mumbai ₹800–2500, Jaipur/Lucknow ₹500–1500, Tier-2 cities ₹400–1000. Budget: ₹400–700, Mid: ₹700–1200, Premium: ₹1200–2000, Luxury: ₹2000+. Peak season (Nov–Feb) mein 10–15% zyada hota hai. Live counters add karne se ₹100–200 per plate extra lagte hain.
500 guests (full dinner buffet, North Indian): Chawal 100 kg, Dal 60 kg, Sabzi 90 kg, Maida/Atta 75 kg, Paneer 22 kg, Sweets/Mithai 40 kg, Raita 50 litre, Salad items 20 kg, Welcome Drink 125 litre. Hamesha 15% extra buffer ke saath order karo — last minute guests guarantee se aate hain!
Complete Indian wedding menu minimum: Welcome drink (2), Starters (3–4), Main course veg (4–5), Non-veg (2–3), Breads (2–3 types), Rice/Biryani, Dal (2 types), Raita + Salad, Desserts (3–4), Papad + Achar, Paan counter. 15–18 items ka spread ideal hai. Live chaat counter additionally add karo — sabse popular hota hai.
Haan! Live counters ke real benefits: (1) Fresh food — taste 10x better hota hai, (2) Entertainment — guests enjoy karte hain dekhke, (3) Premium feel — ₹600 per plate ki shaadi bhi ₹1500 wali lagti hai, (4) Less waste — demand ke hisab se banao, (5) Conversation point — guests yaad rakhte hain. Chaat counter + Paan counter — yeh minimum do hamesha rakhein.
Common caterer scams: (1) "250 guests ke menu" mein 150 pe hi khatam ho jaata hai — Quantity calculator se verify karo, (2) Cheap ingredients mein premium price — paneer milawati, ghee mein dalda, (3) Day-of venue change — advance receipt lo, (4) Overtime charges hidden — contract mein timing clearly likho, (5) Staff extra charges — setup/cleanup included karo contract mein. Solution: Written contract, yahan se Caterer Note copy karo — sab listed hai.
Pure Veg ko boring mat hone do! Tips: (1) Paneer multiple ways — tikka, makhni, kadai, (2) Mushroom + baby corn dishes — non-veg feel, (3) Live chaat counter — sabka favourite, (4) International section — pasta, pizza, (5) Premium sweets — 6–7 varieties, (6) Mocktail bar — colorful presentation, (7) Dry fruits garnish everywhere — premium look. Gujarati/Rajasthani cuisine add karo — naturally veg aur delicious!
