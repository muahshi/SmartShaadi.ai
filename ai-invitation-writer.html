💡 Perfect Wedding Invitation Likhne Ke 5 Tips

  • ✍️
    Dono families ka naam zaroori hai — Indian tradition mein parents ke naam include karna bahut important hai, especially formal invitations mein.
  • 📅
    Date aur time clearly likho — confusion se bachne ke liye date ko full likho (e.g. "15 February 2026, Ravivar") — sirf number se confusion ho sakti hai.
  • 📍
    Venue ka full address do — Google Maps link bhi add karo WhatsApp share mein — zyada logon ko aasani hogi.
  • 💬
    Personal touch add karo — ek custom shayari ya special message field mein apni koi baat likho — AI usse beautifully incorporate kar leta hai.
  • 🔄
    Multiple styles try karo — ek hi details se alag alag styles generate karo aur jo sabse acchi lage woh use karo. Regenerate button free hai!
