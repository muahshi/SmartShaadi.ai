    💡 Wedding Budget Planning Ke 6 Golden Rules

    • 💒
      Venue pe 25-30% se zyada mat lagao — Yeh most common mistake hai. Venue ek hi din ke liye hai, photography memories zindagi bhar ke liye. Balance zaroori hai.
    • 📅
      Off-season mein 20-30% savings — April se September mein book karo — same venue same quality mein 20-30% kam price milega. Best tip hai yeh!
    • 🤝
      Package deals negotiate karo — Venue + catering + decor ek hi vendor se lene par often 10-15% discount milta hai. Negotiate karne mein hichkichao mat.
    • 📱
      Digital invitations se ₹50,000+ bachao — Printed invitation cards pe ₹50,000-₹1,50,000 waste hota hai. AI se banao WhatsApp pe share karo — aur stylish bhi hota hai!
    • 🍽️
      Catering mein local caterer best — Branded caterer se 30-40% mehenga hota hai. Local experienced caterer same quality par kam price mein milega — taste bhi better hoga.
    • 💰
      10% emergency buffer rakho hamesha — Jo bhi budget banao usmein 10% hamesha last-minute emergency ke liye bachao. Last-minute kharche hamesha aate hain!

    Aksar Pooche Jane Wale Sawaal

    Delhi NCR mein ek average Indian wedding (300-400 guests, 2 din, decent venue) ka kharcha ₹15 lakh se ₹35 lakh tak hota hai. Budget wedding ₹8-12 lakh aur grand wedding ₹50 lakh+ ho sakti hai. Peak season (Nov-Feb) mein 20-30% zyada kharcha hota hai.
    Haan bilkul! Tier 2 ya Tier 3 city mein 100-150 guests ke saath ₹5 lakh mein bahut dignified aur beautiful wedding possible hai. Venue ka hall ya lawn rent, simple catering, basic decor, ek photographer — sab fit ho jaata hai. Smart planning se ₹5 lakh mein bhi memorable shaadi hoti hai.
    Top 3 kharche: (1) Venue — 25-35% of total budget, (2) Catering — 20-30%, (3) Decor — 10-15%. Ye teen milake 60-70% budget consume karti hain. Jewelry aur bridal outfit bhi 10-15% le sakti hai. Baaki photography, music, transport mein split hota hai.
    Common hidden costs: Pandit/Maulvi fees, mehendi wali, makeup artist trial, invitation printing + courier, generator backup, parking arrangements, staff tips, last-minute flowers, sound system upgrade, extra photographer hours, relatives ki transport. In sab ke liye 10-15% extra budget rakhna best practice hai.
    Photography pe kanjusi mat karo — memories permanent hain! Budget wedding: ₹30,000-70,000 (local photographer), Mid range: ₹70,000-1,50,000 (experienced with full day coverage + album), Premium: ₹1,50,000-4,00,000 (top photographer, drone, same-day edit). Overall budget ka 8-12% photography pe recommended hai.
    April se September (off-season) mein 20-35% savings hoti hai. June-August mein sabse zyada discount milte hain kyunki venues khali rehte hain. Weekday weddings bhi 10-20% saste hote hain compared to weekends. Off-season choice sabse smart financial decision hai shaadi planning mein.
