1. Venue & Location Hacks

Venue ka rent aapke budget ka 40% kha jata hai. Ise kam karne ke smart tarike:

Hoshangabad Road vs Lake View: Agar aap Bhopal mein hain, toh Lake View ke premium venues ke bajaye Misrod ya Sehore Road ke farmhouses mein 50% tak rent kam ho sakta hai.
Off-Peak Discount: Summer weddings (April-June) mein AC venues par heavy negotiation karein kyunki ye 'Low Demand' season hota hai.

2. Day vs Night: The Financial Battle

Timing badalne se aapka bill badal jata hai:

  • Day Wedding: Lighting ka kharcha 70% kam ho jata hai. Guests appetizers kam khate hain, jo catering bill ko 15% tak niche laata hai.
  • Night Wedding: Generators, heavy ambient lighting, aur extended bar counters aapke bill ko premium side le jate hain.
