1. Venue Hacks: Location par Bachat

Venue ka rent shaadi ke budget ka sabse bada hissa hota hai. Isse aise kam karein:

Tip: Shehar ke center ke bajaye outskirts mein venue dhoondein. Sirf 10-15km ka gap aapka rent 40% kam kar sakta hai.
Tip: Banquet hall ke bajaye bade farmhouse ya open gardens prefer karein jahan vendor monopoly na ho.

2. Date Selection Tricks

India mein "Saaya Dates" par rates double ho jaate hain. Agar aap thode flexible hain, toh ye try karein:

  • Weekday Weddings: Monday se Thursday ke beech venue aur makeup artists 20-30% tak saste milte hain.
  • Off-Season Advantage: July-August ya January ke end mein wedding plan karke aap heavy discounts le sakte hain.
