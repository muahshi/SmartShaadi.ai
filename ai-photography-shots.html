Must-Have Wedding Shots — Reference Guide

💄 Bridal Prep Essentials
  • Lehenga flat-lay detail shot
  • Jewelry close-up — necklace, maang tikka
  • Makeup artist applying kajal
  • Bride looking in mirror (candid)
  • Bride + bridesmaids laughing
  • Hands with mehendi + ring close-up
💍 Ceremony Must-Shots
  • Varmala exchange — wide + close
  • Pheras — each phera separately
  • Sindoor daan — extreme close-up
  • Ring exchange — fingers close-up
  • Pandit Ji + fire — atmospheric
  • Parents watching — emotion capture
😢 Vidai Emotional Shots
  • Bride hugging mother — tears moment
  • Father's last hug before car
  • Rice throwing from car window
  • Groom holding bride's hand — car window
  • Family waving goodbye — wide shot
  • Bride looking back through window
🌅 Golden Hour Magic
  • Silhouette shot against sunset
  • Backlit couple portrait — lehenga flowing
  • Looking away — candid profile
  • Running/walking shot — motion blur
  • Close-up faces — warm light
  • Wide establishing shot — full venue

⚠️ Common Photography Mistakes — Jo Regret Karaati Hain

❌ Shot List Nahi Di
Photographer ne "signature" moments miss kar diye kyunki unhein pata hi nahi tha. Written list dena mandatory hai.
❌ Golden Hour Skip Kiya
Reception ke guests mein busy hoke sunset miss ho gaya. 30 minute ke outdoor shots zindagi bhar yaad rehte hain.
❌ Vidai Rush Kiya
Car mein jaldi bitha diya — photographer ko position lene ka time nahi mila. Sabse emotional moment miss.
❌ Family List Nahi Di
Naani ka portrait, dono families ek saath — yeh shots baad mein yaad aayi jab bohot der ho chuki thi.
❌ Bridal Prep Ignore Ki
"Itna khaas kya hai getting ready mein?" — Actually yeh most emotional aur intimate shots hote hain albums mein.
❌ Only Posed, No Candid
Sirf posed photos lene ka bol diya — real emotions, candid laughter, tears — sab miss. Mix hona chahiye.

📸 Photographer Se Best Kaam Kaise Nikaalein — 6 Tips

📋
List 3 din pehle bhejo, din pe nahi — Photographer ko prepare karne ka time chahiye. Location scout, lighting check, backup plan — sab list milne ke baad hota hai. Day-of list useless hai.
😢
Vidai ke liye dedicated photographer rakhein — Agar 2 photographers hain toh ek sirf Vidai ke liye assign karo. Yeh 20-minute window sabse emotional hoti hai — miss mat hone do.
🌅
Golden hour ke liye 30 min block karo — Reception mein guests hote hain — couple ko 30 min nikaalna hi padega. Calendar mein "6:00-6:30 PM — Couple Portraits Only" likh do.
👨‍👩‍👧
Family list separately banaao — specific names ke saath — "Family photos" nahi — "Bride ke parents + siblings, Groom ke Nana-Nani, Dono families ek saath." Photographer ko exact combinations pata hone chahiye.
💍
Detail shots ke liye props ready rakhein — Jewelry, shoes, invitation card, flowers, ring box — sab ek jagah rakhein. Photographer ko dhundhne nahi padenge aur shots better honge.
🤳
Phone-free zone declare karo rituals mein — Relatives ke phones aur Uncle Ji ka iPad photographer ke shots mein aa jaata hai. Pheras + Sindoor daan mein announce karo — "Please phones down."

Aksar Pooche Jane Wale Sawaal

Good photographers 6-12 months pehle book ho jaate hain — especially peak season (Nov-Feb) mein. Minimum 6 months pehle contact karo. Budget photographers 3-4 months mein available hote hain. Portfolio zaroor dekho aur past clients se baat karo.
Budget range: Entry level ₹30,000-70,000, Mid-range ₹70,000-1,50,000, Premium ₹1,50,000-3,50,000, Top tier ₹3,50,000+. Photography pe zyada invest karo — venue decoration fades, food ends, but photos FOREVER rehte hain. Total budget ka 8-12% photography pe recommended hai.

300+ guests aur 2+ functions: 2 photographers minimum. Ek main photographer (couple + rituals focus) + ek candid specialist (guests + emotions). 500+ guests ya 3+ functions: 3 photographers ideal. Budget mein nahi hai? Ek experienced photographer + ek assistant/second shooter 40-50% sasta option hai.
Standard delivery: 3-6 weeks for edited photos. Premium photographers: 4-8 weeks. Album delivery: 2-4 months extra. Contract mein delivery timeline clearly likhwaao. "Sneak peek" (20-30 preview photos) generally 48-72 hours mein milte hain. Highlight reel video: 4-6 weeks.
